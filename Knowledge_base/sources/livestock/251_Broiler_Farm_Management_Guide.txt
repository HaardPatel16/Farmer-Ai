1. INTRODUCTION
Broiler farming in Gujarat has grown rapidly due to increasing demand for chicken meat. Gujarat produces over 1000 million broilers annually.

2. BREED SELECTION
Commercial Broiler Strains:
- Cobb 400/500/700
- Ross 308/AP95
- Hubbard Classic/Premium
- Arbor Acres Plus

Selection Criteria:
- Fast growth (2 kg in 35-40 days)
- Feed conversion ratio <1.7
- Low mortality (<5%)
- Good meat yield
- Disease resistance

3. HOUSING REQUIREMENTS
Orientation: East-West length for minimal heat
Dimensions:
- Width: 30-40 feet (9-12 m)
- Length: As per capacity
- Height: 12-14 feet (3.5-4 m) at center
- Overhang: 3-4 feet (1 m)

Flooring:
- Concrete preferred
- 6 inch deep litter
- Litter material: Paddy husk, wood shavings

Space Requirements:
- 0.8-1.0 sq ft/bird (summer)
- 0.7-0.8 sq ft/bird (winter)
- 1000 birds need 800-1000 sq ft

Environmental Control:
- Temperature: 33C day 1, reduce 2-3C weekly
- Humidity: 60-70%
- Ventilation: 1 cfm/bird
- Lighting: 23 hours first week, then 20 hours

4. CHICK PROCUREMENT
Source: Reputed hatcheries
Quality Checks:
- Active, alert chicks
- Dry, fluffy feathers
- Clean navels
- Uniform size
- Good hatchery hygiene record

Transportation:
- Ventilated vehicles
- Travel time < 8 hours
- Temperature: 28-30C
- Unload immediately on arrival

5. BROODING MANAGEMENT
Types:
- Spot brooding: 250-500 chicks per hover
- Whole house brooding: For larger farms

Brooder Specifications:
- Hover: 1 hover per 500 chicks
- Height: 18-24 inches adjustable
- Temperature: 95F (35C) first week
- Reduce: 5F (2-3C) per week
- Fuel: LPG/electric/kerosene

Brooding Preparation:
- Clean and disinfect house
- Litter: 3-4 inch deep
- Brooder guard: 3 feet from hover
- Feeders/waterers: 1 per 50 chicks
- Paper: First 3 days for feeding
- Temperature check: 24 hours before

6. FEEDING PROGRAM
Pre-starter (0-7 days):
- Crumbs/mini-pellets
- 23% protein, 3000 kcal ME
- Feed on paper first 3 days

Starter (8-21 days):
- Crumbs/mash
- 22% protein, 3100 kcal ME

Finisher (22-35+ days):
- Pellets/mash
- 20% protein, 3200 kcal ME

Feed Calculation:
- 0-2 weeks: 500 g/bird
- 3-4 weeks: 1500 g/bird
- 5-6 weeks: 3000 g/bird
- Total: 3.5-4 kg/bird to market

Water Management:
- Clean, cool water always
- 1.5-2 x feed intake
- 1000 birds: 80-100 liters/day
- Sanitize water lines weekly

7. VACCINATION SCHEDULE
Day 1: Marek's (at hatchery)
Day 4-5: Newcastle (NDV) - eye drop
Day 7-10: IBD (Gumboro) - eye drop
Day 14-16: NDV booster - drinking water
Day 18-21: IBD booster - drinking water
Day 24-28: NDV + IB - drinking water
Optional: Coccidiosis vaccine at day 1

8. BIOSECURITY
External:
- Fencing around farm
- Footbath at entrance
- Vehicle spray
- Visitor log
- No other poultry within 1 km

Internal:
- All-in all-out system
- Downtime: 10-14 days between batches
- Rodent control
- Wild bird proofing
- Equipment disinfection
- Staff hygiene protocols

9. DAILY MANAGEMENT CHECKLIST
Morning:
- Check temperature and humidity
- Remove dead birds
- Check feed and water
- Observe bird behavior
- Record mortality

Afternoon:
- Adjust ventilation
- Check water quality
- Walk through house
- Record temperature

Evening:
- Ensure adequate feed
- Check water availability
- Secure house
- Final mortality check

10. PERFORMANCE TARGETS
- Body weight: 2.0-2.2 kg at 35 days
- FCR: 1.65-1.75
- Mortality: <5%
- European Efficiency Factor (EEF): >300

11. ECONOMICS
Investment (1000 birds):
- Shed: Rs. 3-5 lakhs (permanent)
- Equipment: Rs. 50,000-80,000
- Chicks: Rs. 40,000-50,000
- Feed: Rs. 2,00,000-2,50,000
- Total working: Rs. 3,00,000-3,50,000

Returns (1000 birds):
- Live birds sold: 950 x 2 kg x Rs. 80/kg = Rs. 1,52,000
- Litter manure: Rs. 10,000
- Gross: Rs. 1,62,000

Note: Broiler economics fluctuates. Integrated companies provide chicks, feed, medicine and buy back birds.

12. MARKETING
Channels:
- Live bird markets
- Processing plants
- Integrated companies (contract)
- Direct to retailers
- Online platforms

Pricing:
- Farm gate: Rs. 70-100/kg live weight
- Retail: Rs. 150-250/kg dressed
- Weekly fluctuation based on supply

13. IMPORTANT CONTACTS
- Gujarat Poultry Farmers Association
- Venky's, Suguna, Skylark (integrators)
- Veterinary colleges
- Feed manufacturers
- KVK poultry units
