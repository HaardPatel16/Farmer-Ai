Kharif 2024 Pest Alert Gujarat — Common pest pressures observed: (1) Pink bollworm (Pectinophora gossypiella) in cotton: Very high pheromone trap catches reported across Saurashtra especially in Rajkot, Amreli, Bhavnagar districts from August onwards. Recommended: use Bt cotton only, set pheromone traps at 45-50 days after sowing (1 trap per acre), spray Indoxacarb 1ml per litre or Chlorantraniliprole (Coragen) 0.3ml per litre from September. (2) Stem fly in soybean: Reported early in July in Banaskantha and Sabarkantha. Seeds treated with Imidacloprid 70 WS at 7g per kg seed shows 80% reduction. (3) Yellow mosaic virus in moong and urd: Transmitted by whitefly in Kutch and North Gujarat — spray Thiamethoxam 0.3g per litre at 10-12 day intervals; remove and destroy infected plants. (4) Leaf folder in paddy (Surat-Navsari belt): Spray Chlorpyriphos 2ml per litre at 20% leaf damage. (5) Fall armyworm in maize: Watch for frass (sawdust-like droppings) in leaf whorl — spray Emamectin 0.4g per litre in whorl using pointed nozzle.
