Kharif crop spray calendar Gujarat (integrated schedule): JUNE-JULY (Early season, sowing to 30 days): Focus on soil-borne diseases and initial sucking pest control. Cotton: seed treatment Imidacloprid 70WS 5g per kg; Groundnut: seed treatment Thiram 3g + Rhizobium; Bajra: seed treatment Metalaxyl for downy mildew prevention. JULY-AUGUST (Vegetative stage, 30-60 days): Cotton: Imidacloprid 0.3ml per litre for jassid/aphid/thrips at 30 days; Mancozeb 2.5g per litre for leaf spot if needed. Groundnut: Mancozeb 2.5g per litre for tikka leaf spot from 30-35 days; Quinalphos 2ml per litre for leaf miner if 5+ mines per leaf. Bajra: Carbofuran 3G in whorl if dead heart (stem borer). AUGUST-SEPTEMBER (Flowering and fruiting, 60-90 days): Cotton: set pheromone traps for pink bollworm; spray Spinosad 0.3ml per litre for bollworm; spray Profenophos 2ml per litre for sucking pests complex. Groundnut: continue Mancozeb 2.5g per litre; foliar boron 0.2% at pod fill. SEPTEMBER-OCTOBER (Pod/boll maturity): Cotton: spray Chlorantraniliprole 0.3ml per litre for late season bollworm; stop spraying 3 weeks before harvest. Groundnut: stop spraying 30 days before harvest. Always mix Sticker (spreader) 0.5ml per litre with any spray; spray in early morning or evening only; wear gloves and mask.
