================================================================================
CASTOR CROP CALENDAR — GUJARAT — JULY
================================================================================

CROP: Castor
MONTH: July
PRIMARY REGION: Saurashtra, North Gujarat
DOCUMENT TYPE: Month-wise Activity Calendar

================================================================================
1. SOIL PREPARATION FOR JULY
================================================================================
  • Light soils (sandy loam) ideal for castor; deep ploughing 20-25 cm before monsoon
  • Apply FYM 5-10 tonnes per hectare before sowing
  • Maintain soil pH 6.0-7.5; castor tolerates poor soils but responds to good drainage
  • Ensure drainage — castor is sensitive to waterlogging; prepare ridges in heavy soils

================================================================================
2. SOWING / PLANTING DATES
================================================================================
  • Sow with monsoon rains in Saurashtra and North Gujarat rainfed tracts
  • Seed rate: 5-6 kg per hectare for hybrid varieties (e.g., GCH-4, GCH-7); 8-10 kg for non-hybrid
  • Spacing: 120cm x 60cm for hybrids; 90cm x 45cm for non-hybrids
  • Wider spacing than most kharif crops due to large plant canopy
  • Treat seeds with Carbendazim 2g per kg to prevent seedling diseases

================================================================================
3. IRRIGATION SCHEDULE
================================================================================
  • Generally rainfed; one irrigation at 30-40 days if dry spell occurs during vegetative growth
  • Second irrigation at 50-60 days during flowering if no rain
  • In irrigated tracts, drip irrigation with 4 litre/hour emitters can be used
  • Ensure drainage after heavy rain — waterlogging causes root rot and sudden wilt

================================================================================
4. FERTILIZER TIMING & DOSES
================================================================================
  • Basal dose at sowing: DAP 100 kg + potash 30 kg per hectare
  • First nitrogen top dressing (40 kg urea per hectare) at 30 days after sowing
  • Second nitrogen top dressing (40 kg urea per hectare) at 60 days after sowing
  • Micronutrients: apply zinc sulphate 25 kg per hectare as basal if soil is deficient

================================================================================
5. PEST SCOUTING DATES
================================================================================
  • Castor shoot and capsule borer (Conogethes punctiferalis): monitor for webbed shoots; remove and destroy infested shoots
  • Castor semilooper (Achaea janata): if larvae exceed 5 per plant, spray Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt) 1g per litre
  • Jassids: if damage exceeds 5% leaf area, spray Imidacloprid 0.5ml per litre
  • Whitefly: use yellow sticky traps; spray Buprofezin 1.5ml per litre if threshold crossed
  • Root rot: if waterlogging occurs, drench with Copper oxychloride 2.5g per litre + Metalaxyl 2.5g per litre

================================================================================
6. HARVESTING WINDOWS
================================================================================
  • NO harvest in July — castor is in vegetative stage (20-40 days)
  • Continue monitoring crop growth and pest populations
  • Record keeping: document sowing date and variety for harvest planning (October-November)

================================================================================
7. POST-HARVEST OPERATIONS
================================================================================
  • NO post-harvest operations in July
  • Prepare storage facilities and threshing equipment for October-November harvest
  • Contact oil mills or market yards for price information and procurement schedules

================================================================================
8. WEATHER WATCH
================================================================================
  • Peak monsoon month: heavy rainfall in Saurashtra; 250-350mm average in July
  • Waterlogging risk; check drainage channels after every rain event
  • Humidity 70-90%; favourable for fungal diseases — increase scouting frequency
  • Day temperatures 28-32°C; nights 24-26°C; warm and humid
  • IMD warnings: watch for heavy rainfall alerts and flood warnings

================================================================================
9. GOVERNMENT SCHEME DEADLINES
================================================================================
  • PMFBY enrollment: complete by 31st July for Kharif castor
  • Castor seed subsidy: purchase certified seeds from authorized dealers with bill
  • Kisan Credit Card (KCC): renew or apply for fresh credit before sowing season
  • Gujarat State Oilseeds Mission: contact district agriculture office for technical support and subsidies
  • Contact KVK for improved varieties and cultivation techniques
