1. INTRODUCTION
Gujarat has a diverse climate ranging from arid in the northwest to tropical wet in the south. Understanding the state's climate is essential for successful farming.

2. CLIMATE CLASSIFICATION
Based on Koppen Classification:
- BWh (Hot desert): Kutch, North Gujarat
- BSh (Hot semi-arid): Saurashtra, North Gujarat
- Aw (Tropical savanna): South Gujarat
- Am (Tropical monsoon): Dangs, Valsad

3. SEASONAL PATTERN
Summer (March-June):
- Temperature: 35-48C
- Highest: 45-48C in Kutch, North Gujarat
- Heat waves: April-May
- Irrigation demand: Peak

Monsoon (June-September):
- Onset: Second week of June (South Gujarat)
- Peak: July-August
- Withdrawal: September end
- Distribution: Uneven across state

Post-Monsoon (October-November):
- Temperature: 25-35C
- Humidity: Moderate
- Best for Rabi planting

Winter (December-February):
- Temperature: 10-30C
- Lowest: 5-10C in North Gujarat
- Frost risk: Minimal except hills
- Best for vegetable production

4. AGRO-CLIMATIC ZONES
Zone 1 - South Gujarat:
- Rainfall: 1200-2000 mm
- Crops: Rice, sugarcane, mango, chikoo

Zone 2 - Middle Gujarat:
- Rainfall: 800-1000 mm
- Crops: Cotton, wheat, cumin, bajra

Zone 3 - North Gujarat:
- Rainfall: 500-700 mm
- Crops: Castor, cotton, wheat, mustard

Zone 4 - Saurashtra:
- Rainfall: 400-700 mm
- Crops: Groundnut, cotton, bajra, sesame

Zone 5 - Kutch:
- Rainfall: 300-400 mm
- Crops: Date palm, bajra, guar fodder

5. CLIMATE CHANGE IMPACTS
Observed Changes:
- Increasing temperature: 0.5C per decade
- Erratic rainfall: More intense but fewer rainy days
- Delayed monsoon: Shifted by 7-10 days
- Increased drought frequency

Projected Impacts:
- 10-20% reduction in wheat yield by 2050
- Increased pest/disease pressure
- Water scarcity intensification
- Coastal salinity intrusion

6. ADAPTATION STRATEGIES
Short-term:
- Weather-based agro-advisories
- Crop insurance
- Contingency plans

Medium-term:
- Climate-resilient varieties
- Water harvesting
- Crop diversification

Long-term:
- Changing cropping patterns
- Protected cultivation expansion
- Integrated farming systems
