Morbi district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: Central Saurashtra Zone. Soil type: medium black soil, some saline patches near Little Rann. Annual rainfall: low, 500-600mm annual. Major crops: cotton, groundnut, wheat, bajra. Irrigation: Machhu dam system and borewells, ceramics industry growth has increased competing water demand. Notes: known nationally for its ceramics industry, agriculture and industry compete for groundwater.
