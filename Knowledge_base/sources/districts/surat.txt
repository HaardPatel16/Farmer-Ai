Surat district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: South Gujarat Zone. Soil type: deep black soil and alluvial soil near river belts. Annual rainfall: high, 1200-1400mm annual. Major crops: sugarcane, paddy (rice), banana, cotton, vegetables. Irrigation: Ukai-Kakrapar canal system, one of the best-irrigated districts in Gujarat. Notes: sugarcane belt with multiple sugar cooperative mills, high rainfall supports double cropping.
