Surendranagar district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: Central Saurashtra Zone. Soil type: medium black soil, some saline patches near Little Rann of Kutch. Annual rainfall: low, 500-600mm annual, drought-prone. Major crops: cotton, groundnut, bajra, wheat. Irrigation: Limited canal command from Bhogavo/Demi dams, mostly rainfed, drought-prone district historically. Notes: one of the more drought-prone districts in Saurashtra, water harvesting structures important.
