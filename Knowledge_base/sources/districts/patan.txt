Patan district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: North Gujarat Zone. Soil type: sandy and saline soil near Rann, sandy loam elsewhere. Annual rainfall: low, 500-600mm annual. Major crops: bajra, castor, cumin, wheat, cotton. Irrigation: Narmada canal (Sujalam Sufalam) reaching parts, groundwater often saline near the Rann. Notes: northern talukas bordering Rann of Kutch face soil and water salinity challenges.
