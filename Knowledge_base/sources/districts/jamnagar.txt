Jamnagar district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: North Saurashtra coastal Zone. Soil type: medium black to coastal sandy soil. Annual rainfall: low to moderate, 550-650mm annual. Major crops: groundnut, cotton, wheat, bajra. Irrigation: Und and Sasoi dam systems, borewells, coastal salinity ingress a long-standing issue. Notes: major groundnut oil processing hub, coastal industrial belt (refineries) competes for water and land.
