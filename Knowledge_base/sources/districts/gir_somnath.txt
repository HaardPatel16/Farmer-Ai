Gir Somnath district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: South Saurashtra coastal Zone. Soil type: medium black soil, coastal sandy near shore. Annual rainfall: good, 900-1000mm annual. Major crops: groundnut, mango (Gir Kesar), cotton, coconut (coastal talukas). Irrigation: Hiran and Shingoda river systems, relatively better water availability than central Saurashtra. Notes: shares the Gir forest Kesar mango belt with Junagadh, carved from Junagadh and Amreli districts.
