Tapi district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: South Gujarat Zone. Soil type: medium black to lateritic in hilly tracts. Annual rainfall: high, 1300-1500mm annual. Major crops: paddy (rice), sugarcane, cotton, tur (pigeon pea), banana. Irrigation: Ukai reservoir is located in this district, canal command in plains, rainfed in hilly talukas. Notes: Ukai dam (on the Tapi river) supplies irrigation to much of South Gujarat, carved from Surat district.
