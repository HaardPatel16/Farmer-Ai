Navsari district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: South Gujarat Zone. Soil type: deep fertile alluvial and black soil. Annual rainfall: very high, 1800-2000mm annual. Major crops: sugarcane, paddy (rice), banana, mango (Alphonso/Kesar), chikoo. Irrigation: Purna and Ambika river systems, generally water-abundant district. Notes: high rainfall and fertile soil support intensive horticulture, well known for mango and chikoo orchards.
