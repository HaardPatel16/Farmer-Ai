Mehsana district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: North Gujarat Zone. Soil type: sandy loam. Annual rainfall: low to moderate, 600-700mm annual. Major crops: potato, castor, bajra, wheat, cumin. Irrigation: heavy tubewell dependence, groundwater overexploited in several talukas, Sujalam Sufalam canal helps recharge in parts. Notes: second major potato belt after Banaskantha, also a strong dairy region.
