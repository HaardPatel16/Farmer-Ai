Sabarkantha district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: North Gujarat Zone. Soil type: sandy loam to medium black in river valleys. Annual rainfall: moderate, 700-800mm annual. Major crops: wheat, bajra, maize, groundnut, vegetables. Irrigation: Hathmati and Dharoi dam canal systems, tubewells. Notes: hilly eastern talukas suit maize and minor millets, western plains suit wheat and groundnut.
