Junagadh district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: South Saurashtra Zone. Soil type: medium black soil, coastal sandy soil near Gir. Annual rainfall: good, 900-1000mm annual, highest in Saurashtra. Major crops: groundnut, cotton, mango (Gir belt), wheat. Irrigation: check dams and Hiran/Ozat river systems, groundwater generally better than central Saurashtra. Notes: famous for Kesar mango orchards around Gir forest, groundnut processing hub.
