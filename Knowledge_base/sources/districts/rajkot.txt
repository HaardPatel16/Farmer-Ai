Rajkot district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: Saurashtra Zone. Soil type: medium black soil (vertisol). Annual rainfall: moderate, 550-650mm annual. Major crops: groundnut, cotton, wheat, bajra. Irrigation: borewell-dependent, declining groundwater table is a major concern, check dams help recharge. Notes: groundnut is the dominant cash crop, major mandi hub for groundnut and cotton trading.
