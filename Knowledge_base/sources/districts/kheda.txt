Kheda district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: Middle Gujarat Zone. Soil type: Goradu (sandy loam), fertile and well-drained. Annual rainfall: moderate, 800-900mm annual. Major crops: tobacco, paddy (rice), wheat, banana, vegetables. Irrigation: Mahi canal command, good groundwater, well irrigated district. Notes: historically the cradle of Gujarat's dairy cooperative movement alongside Anand.
