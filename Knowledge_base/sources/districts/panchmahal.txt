Panchmahal district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: Middle Gujarat Zone. Soil type: lateritic and medium black soil in hilly/undulating terrain. Annual rainfall: moderate to good, 850-950mm annual. Major crops: maize, paddy (rice), wheat, tur (pigeon pea), cotton. Irrigation: Panam and Kadana dam canal systems, otherwise rainfed in upland tracts. Notes: maize is a dominant kharif crop in tribal upland areas.
