Vadodara district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: Middle Gujarat Zone. Soil type: medium black to alluvial soil along Mahi and Narmada belts. Annual rainfall: moderate to good, 900-1000mm annual. Major crops: cotton, paddy (rice), tur (pigeon pea), maize, vegetables. Irrigation: Mahi canal command and Narmada canal, relatively well irrigated. Notes: diverse cropping due to two major river systems passing through the district.
