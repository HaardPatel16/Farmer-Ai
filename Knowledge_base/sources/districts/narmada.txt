Narmada district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: South Gujarat Zone (tribal belt). Soil type: medium black to lateritic in hilly tracts. Annual rainfall: good, 1000-1100mm annual. Major crops: paddy (rice), maize, tur (pigeon pea), cotton, banana. Irrigation: Sardar Sarovar (Narmada) dam is located in this district, extensive canal network feeds much of Gujarat though local coverage in hilly talukas is uneven. Notes: site of the Sardar Sarovar Dam itself, tribal-majority hilly terrain.
