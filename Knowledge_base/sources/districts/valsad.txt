Valsad district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: South Gujarat Zone. Soil type: deep fertile alluvial soil. Annual rainfall: very high, 2000-2500mm annual, wettest district in Gujarat. Major crops: mango (Alphonso), chikoo, sugarcane, paddy (rice), banana. Irrigation: abundant rainfall and river water, irrigation deficit is rarely the limiting factor here. Notes: premier Alphonso mango belt of Gujarat, horticulture export hub.
