Mahisagar district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: Middle Gujarat Zone (tribal belt). Soil type: lateritic and medium black, undulating terrain. Annual rainfall: moderate to good, 850-950mm annual. Major crops: maize, paddy (rice), tur (pigeon pea), wheat. Irrigation: Kadana dam canal partially covers the district, otherwise rainfed. Notes: carved from Panchmahal district, tribal-majority hilly terrain.
