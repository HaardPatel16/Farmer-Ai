Kutch district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: North-West Arid Zone. Soil type: sandy and saline-coastal soils, some black soil patches inland. Annual rainfall: scanty, 350-400mm annual, most arid district in Gujarat. Major crops: bajra, castor, cotton, cumin, isabgol, date palm. Irrigation: mostly rainfed; limited canal command from Narmada branch canal reaching eastern Kutch, groundwater largely saline. Notes: groundwater is brackish in most talukas, drip irrigation strongly recommended for any irrigated crop, dryland farming practices essential.
