Porbandar district Gujarat agro-climatic profile. Zone: South Saurashtra coastal Zone. Soil type: coastal sandy and medium black soil. Annual rainfall: low to moderate, 600-700mm annual. Major crops: groundnut, cotton, wheat, onion. Irrigation: limited check dams and borewells, coastal salinity ingress affects groundwater near the coast. Notes: coastal district with fishing economy alongside farming, salinity management important near shore.
