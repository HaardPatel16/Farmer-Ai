1. INTRODUCTION
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) provides income support of Rs. 6,000 per year to all farmer families across India, paid in three installments.

2. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
Inclusions:
- Small and marginal farmers
- All landholding farmers
- Families with cultivable land
- Farmers in both urban and rural areas

Exclusions:
- Institutional landholders
- Farmer families with high income (MPs, MLAs, etc.)
- Retired pensioners above Rs. 10,000/month
- Professionals (doctors, engineers, CAs)
- Income tax payers

3. BENEFIT STRUCTURE
Annual Amount: Rs. 6,000 per family
Installments: Rs. 2,000 every 4 months
Timeline:
- 1st installment: April-July
- 2nd installment: August-November
- 3rd installment: December-March

Mode: Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to bank account

4. REGISTRATION PROCESS
Online Method:
Step 1: Visit pmkisan.gov.in
Step 2: Click "New Farmer Registration"
Step 3: Enter Aadhaar number
Step 4: Fill personal details
Step 5: Enter land details
Step 6: Upload documents
Step 7: Submit and note reference number

Offline Method:
- Visit nearest CSC (Common Service Center)
- Contact Village Level Entrepreneur (VLE)
- Visit Agriculture Department office
- Through designated bank branches

Required Documents:
- Aadhaar card
- Land records (7/12 or equivalent)
- Bank passbook (linked to Aadhaar)
- Mobile number
- Passport size photo

5. EKYC REQUIREMENT
Mandatory for all beneficiaries:
- Biometric authentication
- OTP-based verification
- Face authentication
- Can be done at CSC, bank, or online

6. STATUS CHECK
Methods:
- Online: pmkisan.gov.in (Beneficiary Status)
- SMS: PMKISAN <Aadhaar> to 51969
- Mobile app: PM-KISAN app
- Helpline: 155261 / 011-24300606

Common Status:
- FTO Generated: Ready for payment
- PFMS Confirmed: Payment processed
- Status Active: Eligible for next installment
- Rejected: Correct errors and reapply

7. COMMON ISSUES AND SOLUTIONS
Problem: Account not credited
Solution: Check DBT status, ensure Aadhaar-bank linking

Problem: Land record mismatch
Solution: Update records at Revenue Department

Problem: Exclusion category
Solution: Check exclusion list, appeal if incorrect

Problem: Name mismatch
Solution: Correct Aadhaar or land records

8. IMPORTANT DATES
Registration: Open throughout the year
Installment releases: As per government schedule
EKYC deadline: Updated on portal regularly

9. IMPORTANT CONTACTS
- Website: pmkisan.gov.in
- Helpline: 155261
- Email: pmkisan-ias@gov.in
- State nodal officer: Gujarat Agriculture Department
