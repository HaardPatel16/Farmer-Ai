MSP Rabi 2025-26 Minimum Support Prices India: Wheat Rs 2275 per quintal. Mustard (Rapeseed) Rs 5950 per quintal. Chana (Gram) Rs 5440 per quintal. Lentil (Masur) Rs 6700 per quintal. Barley Rs 1735 per quintal. Safflower Rs 5800 per quintal. Cumin (Jeera) — not under central MSP but Gujarat has own procurement scheme at Rs 25,000-30,000 per quintal approximate market rate. These MSPs apply for procurement by state and central agencies. Gujarat Rabi crops: wheat MSP procurement happens through FCI/NAFED at designated centres in major wheat districts (Banaskantha, Mehsana, Patan, Anand, Vadodara). Mustard procurement: NAFED and state cooperative agencies procure during March-April at MSP. How to sell at MSP: register at meri fasal mera byora portal (central) or nearest APMC; bring Aadhaar, bank passbook, land records, quality certificate (moisture below 12%, clean grain).
