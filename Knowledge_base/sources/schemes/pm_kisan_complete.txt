PM-KISAN scheme complete guide for Gujarat farmers: PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi) provides Rs 6,000 per year income support to all farmer families with cultivable land. Payment is in three instalments of Rs 2,000 each — April-July, August-November, December-March. Eligibility: all farmers with land records in their name; excludes institutional landholders, former/current constitutional post holders, income taxpayers, and professionals like doctors/lawyers/engineers. Registration: visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or bank with Aadhar card, land records (7-12 utara or Pahani), and bank passbook. Or register online at pmkisan.gov.in. Registration is free — do not pay anyone. Check payment status: pmkisan.gov.in → "Beneficiary Status" → enter Aadhar or mobile number. Common problems and solutions: payment not received — check beneficiary status; if "Payment Pending" means state verification pending — contact district agriculture office. Name mismatch between Aadhar and land record — apply for name correction at Aadhar centre first. Bank account number wrong — update at nearest bank branch with PM-KISAN correction form. Land record not verified — contact Circle Inspector (CI) or taluka mamlatdar. Exclusion error (wrongly excluded) — submit grievance at pmkisan.gov.in under "Help Desk" or call 155261. Gujarat context: approximately 55-60 lakh Gujarat farmers are PM-KISAN beneficiaries; over Rs 2,500 crore disbursed to Gujarat farmers annually. New farmers: PM-KISAN new registration stopped temporarily after 2022 due to database cleaning — check current status at pmkisan.gov.in or CSC as policy changes occur.
