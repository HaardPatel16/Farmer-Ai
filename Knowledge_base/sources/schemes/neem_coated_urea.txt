Neem Coated Urea subsidy and benefits Gujarat: Government of India mandates all domestic urea to be neem coated since 2015. Neem coating reduces nitrogen loss from soil by 10-15% (slows nitrification) leading to higher nitrogen use efficiency. Benefits to farmers: (1) Effective fertilizer dose can be reduced by 10-15% with similar yield. (2) Insecticidal properties of neem reduces certain soil pests. (3) Reduces leaching of nitrogen in sandy soils (important in Kutch and North Gujarat). Price: same as regular urea (subsidised price Rs 266.50 per 45kg bag). Identification: neem coated urea bags have green-coloured printing and "Neem Coated" label. Important: neem coated urea cannot be diverted for industrial use — this is government measure to ensure subsidy benefits reach farmers. Available through GSSC (Gujarat State Seeds Corporation) dealers and cooperative societies. Always purchase with bill to claim subsidy benefits.
