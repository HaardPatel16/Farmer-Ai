PM PRANAM Scheme (PM Programme for Restoration, Awareness, Nourishment and Amelioration of Mother Earth): Central government initiative launched 2023 to reduce chemical fertilizer use in India. Incentivises states to reduce fertilizer subsidy expenditure by promoting alternative nutrients and organic farming. How it helps farmers: (1) States get 50% of saved subsidy funds to spend on farmer incentives for alternative fertilizer adoption. (2) Gujarat farmers reducing chemical fertilizer usage and shifting to bio-fertilizers, organic inputs eligible for special grants. (3) Promotes use of nano urea (liquid), nano DAP, biostimulants, soil health improvement. Nano Urea: IFFCO's nano urea liquid — 500ml bottle equivalent to one 45kg urea bag for foliar spray; spray 3-4ml per litre at tillering and flowering stage; reduces soil nitrogen pollution and cost. Contact: Agriculture department, ikhedut portal, or IFFCO representative for nano urea availability in Gujarat.
