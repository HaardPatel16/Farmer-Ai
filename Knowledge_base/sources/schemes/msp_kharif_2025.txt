MSP Kharif 2025 Minimum Support Prices India (announced by CCEA): Groundnut (in shell) Rs 6783 per quintal. Cotton (Medium staple) Rs 7121 per quintal. Cotton (Long staple) Rs 7521 per quintal. Bajra (Pearl millet) Rs 2625 per quintal. Maize Rs 2225 per quintal. Soybean (Yellow) Rs 4892 per quintal. Sesame (Til) Rs 9267 per quintal. Sunflower seed Rs 7280 per quintal. Castor seed Rs 6589 per quintal. Moong (Green gram) Rs 8682 per quintal. Urad (Black gram) Rs 7400 per quintal. Tur (Arhar/Pigeon pea) Rs 7550 per quintal. Rice (Paddy) Rs 2300 per quintal. Jowar (Hybrid) Rs 3371 per quintal. These are minimum floor prices — farmers are entitled to receive at least these prices when selling to government procurement agencies or APMC mandis. Contact district marketing officer or APMC for procurement schedule in your area.
