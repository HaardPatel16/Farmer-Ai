AQUACULTURE IN ANAND DISTRICT — GUJARAT
=========================================

OVERVIEW
Anand district in Middle Gujarat has good potential for freshwater aquaculture due to 
the Mahi River basin, canal networks, and seasonal ponds. Fish farming provides 
supplementary income alongside the district's dominant dairy and crop farming economy.

SUITABLE SPECIES
- Rohu (Labeo rohita): Fast-growing surface/mid-water feeder; 1-2 kg in 10-12 months
- Catla (Catla catla): Surface feeder; reaches 2-3 kg in 12 months
- Mrigal (Cirrhinus mrigala): Bottom feeder; completes polyculture system
- Common Carp (Cyprinus carpio): Hardy; tolerates variable water quality
- Grass Carp: Controls aquatic weeds naturally

Recommended Polyculture Ratio (per hectare):
Catla 20% + Rohu 40% + Mrigal 20% + Common Carp 20%
Stocking density: 8,000–10,000 fingerlings/ha
Expected yield: 3,000–4,000 kg/ha/year

POND PREPARATION
1. Dewater and sun-dry pond bottom for 1-2 weeks
2. Apply lime (CaO) @ 200-250 kg/ha to disinfect and adjust pH
3. Apply cow dung @ 2,000-3,000 kg/ha as base manure
4. Fill to 1.5-2 m depth; wait 2 weeks before stocking
5. Target water quality: pH 7-8.5, Secchi disc transparency 25-35 cm

FEEDING AND MANAGEMENT
- Feed: rice bran + mustard cake (1:1) @ 2-3% body weight daily
- Monthly fertilisation: urea 20 kg/ha + SSP 25 kg/ha for plankton growth
- Feed twice daily (morning and evening); reduce during monsoon
- Change 20-25% water monthly in summer

HARVEST AND MARKETING
- Partial harvest from 6-8 months for marketable fish
- Full harvest at 12 months using drag nets
- Anand, Petlad, Khambhat fish markets — daily sales
- Prices: ₹80-200/kg depending on species and season

ECONOMICS (1 HECTARE / YEAR)
- Investment (pond prep + fingerlings + feed + labour): ₹1.5-2.0 lakh
- Income (3,000-4,000 kg @ ₹80-100/kg avg): ₹2.5-3.5 lakh
- Net profit: ₹80,000–1.5 lakh per hectare

GOVERNMENT SUPPORT
- PM Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY): 40-60% subsidy on pond construction
- SC/ST farmers: 60% subsidy on inputs
- Subsidised fingerlings from Gujarat Fisheries Dept
- Apply: ikhedut.gujarat.gov.in
