=== PATHOLOGICAL MANAGEMENT FOR GROUNDNUT ===
Target Threats: Tikka Leaf Spot, Rust, and Collar/Stem Rot

1. TIKKA LEAF SPOT (Early & Late Cercospora Spots)
- Manifestation: Circular brown spots with yellow halos spreading across foliage, leading to premature leaf drop and reduced photosynthesis.
- Fungicidal Schedule: Spray Hexaconazole 5% EC @ 15 ml or Tebuconazole 25.9% EC @ 10 ml per 10 liters of water at 45 and 60 days after sowing.

2. STEM ROT (Sclerotium rolfsii)
- Manifestation: White, web-like fungal mycelium appearing at the soil-stem interface, followed by rapid rotting of the collar region and pod shedding.
- Biocontrol Protocol: Apply Trichoderma harzianum @ 2.5 kg mixed with 500 kg of well-decomposed organic manure or castor cake per hectare during the final interculturing phase.
