=== IRRIGATED WHEAT (GHAU) MANAGEMENT DESIGN ===
Source: Wheat Research Station, JAU (Junagadh) & AAU (Anand)
Target Zones: Anand, Kheda, Ahmedabad, Rajkot, and Junagadh

1. SOWING SCHEDULES AND SEED CALIBRATION
- Irrigated Timely Sowing: November 1 to November 25. Spacing: 22.5 cm row distance. Seed rate: 100-120 kg per hectare.
- Irrigated Late Sowing: November 26 to December 25. Spacing: 18 cm row distance. Seed rate: 120-150 kg/ha to compensate for reduced tillering windows.

2. CROP RESIDUE MANAGEMENT
- Avoid burning paddy residues prior to wheat sowing. Incorporate residues into the soil using a rotavator combined with a starter dose of 25 kg Urea per hectare to accelerate decomposition.
