=== BAJRA PATHOLOGY AND ERADICATION ===
1. DOWNY MILDEW (Sclerospora graminicola): Typical 'Green Ear' conversion of healthy grain heads into twisted leaf-like profiles. Treat seeds with Metalaxyl-M 35% WS @ 6g/kg.
2. ERGOT: Managed by skipping infected grains and choosing seed stock cleaned via salt flotation cycles.
