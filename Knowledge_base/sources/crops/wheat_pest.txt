=== WHEAT ENTOMOLOGICAL DEFENCE AND MANAGEMENT ===
Target Pests: Termites (Microtermes obesi) and Aphids

1. TERMITE MANAGEMENT (SOIL INFESTATION CORE)
- Symptoms: Attacks roots and base stems under light soils, causing whole tillers to dry out. Prevalent in fields with poorly decomposed FYM.
- Control Vectors: Treat seeds with Fipronil 5% SC @ 4 ml/kg seed. In standing crops with active infestations, apply Chlorpyrifos 20% EC @ 3-4 liters per hectare mixed with irrigation water or sand.
