=== ELITE CUMIN GENETICS IN GUJARAT ===
Source: SDAU Jagudan Research Station

1. GC-4 (Gujarat Cumin 4)
- Agronomic Profile: Medium-sized bold seeds, aromatic essential oil content ~3.5%. Maturation window: 105-110 days. Shows moderate structural tolerance to Fusarium wilt under standard crop rotation models.

2. GC-5 (High Resistance Selection)
- Agronomic Profile: Custom-bred to counter the severe wilt epidemics in North Gujarat. Features an extended shelf-life profile and a higher density of secondary branches, yielding up to 800-1000 kg/ha under precision management.
