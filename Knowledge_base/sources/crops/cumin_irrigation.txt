=== CUMIN IRRIGATION SCHEDULING (LIGHT TOUCH SYSTEM) ===
Target Ecosystem: Arid and Semi-Arid Sandy Loams of North Gujarat

1. CALIBRATED WATER DELIVERY FLUSHES
Cumin requires 4 to 5 light irrigations in total. Never flood the fields, as excess water triggers immediate fungal infections:
- First Irrigation: Immediately after sowing to trigger germination (very light, controlled flow).
- Second Irrigation: 8-10 days post-sowing to reinforce seedling emergence through the soil crust.
- Third Irrigation: 30-35 days post-sowing (vegetative branching phase).
- Fourth Irrigation: 50-55 days post-sowing (flowering stage).
- Fifth Irrigation: 70-75 days post-sowing (seed-filling stage). Completely halt all water applications past day 80 to ensure seed drying.
