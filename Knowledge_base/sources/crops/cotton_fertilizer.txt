=== COTTON FERTILIZER AND NUTRIENT ARCHITECTURE ===
Source: Anand Agricultural University (AAU) Dosing Directives

1. ORGANIC BASE AMENDMENTS
- Incorporate 10 to 12 metric tonnes of well-decomposed Farm Yard Manure (FYM) or 1.5 metric tonnes of premium castor cake per hectare during final harrowing to optimize soil organic carbon status and enhance CEC (Cation Exchange Capacity).

2. INORGANIC NPK REGIMEN
- Total Chemical Requirement: 240:0:0 kg NPK/ha for Irrigated Bt Hybrids; 80:0:0 kg NPK/ha for Rainfed crops. Phosphorus (P) and Potassium (K) are omitted unless soil testing indicates baseline deficits below 22 kg P2O5/ha and 130 kg K2O/ha.

3. NITROGEN SPLIT SCHEDULE (IRRIGATED BT HYBRIDS)
- Basal Dose: Omit nitrogen at sowing to prevent leaching, or apply a minimal starter dose of 20 kg N/ha only if soil is highly sandy loam.
- First Top-Dressing (Thinning Stage - 30 DAS): Apply 1/3rd dose (80 kg N/ha) using side-dressing methods.
- Second Top-Dressing (Peak Flowering Stage - 60 DAS): Apply 1/3rd dose (80 kg N/ha) deeply placed into furrows.
- Third Top-Dressing (Boll Development Phase - 80 DAS): Apply the final 1/3rd dose (80 kg N/ha) under optimum soil moisture to maximize boll weight and lint ginning turn-out.
