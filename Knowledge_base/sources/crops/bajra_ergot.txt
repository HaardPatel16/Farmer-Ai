=== BAJRA ERGOT (CLAVICEPS FUSIFORMIS) CONTROL PROTOCOLS ===
1. SYMPTOMATOLOGY: Fluid pink droplets ooze from grain spikelets during high humidity, turning into toxic black sclerotia bodies.
2. ERADICATION: Float seeds in 10% NaCl salt solution to remove sclerotia. Spray Propiconazole 25% EC @ 10ml/10L at 5% field flowering.
