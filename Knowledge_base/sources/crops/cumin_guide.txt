=== CUMIN (JEERA) PRODUCTION SYSTEM MATRIX ===
Source: Seed Spices Research Station, SDAU, Jagudan
Target Belts: Mehsana, Banaskantha, Patan, Morbi, Rajkot, and Surendranagar

1. CLIMATIC LIMITATIONS & SOWING WINDOWS
- Strict Climate Constraint: Requires a completely clear, dry winter temperature profile with zero humidity. High cloudiness or unseasonal rain (local term: 'Mavthu') can cause complete crop collapse due to disease vectors.
- Sowing Window: November 1 to November 25. Sowing should commence when ambient day temperatures stabilize between 25-28°C.
- Seed Metrics: 12 to 15 kg per hectare. Sown via broadcasting or light seed drilling at a shallow depth of 1.0 to 1.5 cm. Deep sowing prevents seed emergence.

2. POST-HARVEST HANDLING
- Harvest when plants turn golden-yellow (late February to March). Clear fields completely of weeds before harvest to avoid mixing weed seeds with cumin, preserving purity for the Unjha export terminal.
