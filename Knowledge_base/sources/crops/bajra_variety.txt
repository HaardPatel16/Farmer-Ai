=== GUJARAT BAJRA VARIETAL INDEX ===
Source: Mandor & Jamnagar Pearl Millet Research Stations
1. GHB-558 / GHB-538 (High-Yield Mainstay): Celebrated for strong stem architecture, reducing lodging under sudden late monsoon winds. Yield profile spans 3200-3600 kg/ha.
2. GHB-732 (Summer Heat Champion): Bred to withstand temperatures exceeding 42°C during anthesis in North Gujarat without pollen sterility.
