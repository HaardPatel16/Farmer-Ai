=== IRRIGATED WHEAT VARIETIES IN GUJARAT ===
Source: AAU & JAU Wheat Research Stations

1. GW-496 (The Regional Standard)
- Maturation Profile: 105-112 days. White amber grains, excellent chapati-making qualities (high gluten index). Highly resistant to stem rust vectors.

2. GW-322 / GW-451 (High Yield Super Selections)
- Maturation Profile: 115-120 days. Tall erect growth habit, highly responsive to heavy fertilizer inputs (120:60:40 kg NPK/ha). Produces bold, heavy lustrous grains with excellent market premium value across Gujarat APMCs.
