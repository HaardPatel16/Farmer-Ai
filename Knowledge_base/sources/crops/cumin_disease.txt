=== CUMIN FUSARIUM WILT (SUKARO) ERADICATION MANIFESTO ===
Pathogen Baseline: Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. cumini (Soil-borne vascular fungus)

1. PATHOLOGICAL SYMPTOM PROGRESSION
- Manifests as patch-wise drooping of seedling tips during late January. Leaves rapidly turn yellow-brown, and the vascular bundle inside the root turns blackish-brown. Plants dry up completely, resembling burnt patches.

2. INTEGRATED STEWARDSHIP PROTOCOLS
- Mandatory Biological Pre-Treatment: Treat seeds with Trichoderma viride formulation @ 10 grams/kg. Incorporate 2.5 kg of Trichoderma viride culture blended into 500 kg of vermicompost or castor cake per hectare into the topsoil during final field layout preparation.
- Mandatory Crop Rotation: Enforce a strict 3-year crop rotation. Never grow cumin after cumin. Rotate with Mustard or Rabi Sorghum.
- Field Emergency Remediation: Upon spot tracking of wilt, immediately isolate the zone and drench the soil patch plus a 3-meter safety radius using Carbendazim 50% WP @ 15 grams per 10 liters of water.
