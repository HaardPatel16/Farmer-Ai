=== BAJRA ENTOMOLOGICAL THREAT CONTROLS ===
1. SHOOT FLY (Atherigona soccata): Causes typical dead-heart symptoms in seedlings. Control via seed treatment with Thiamethoxam 30 FS @ 5.5 ml/kg seed.
2. BLISTER BEETLE: Feeds on pollen during flowering. Manually collect via hand nets or spray Deltamethrin 2.8 EC @ 10 ml/10L water.
