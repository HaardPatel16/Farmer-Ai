=== GENETIC PROFILE AND VARIETAL INDEX OF GUJARAT COTTON ===
Database Classification: Junagadh Agricultural University & Navsari Agricultural University

1. G.COT HYBRID 6 / 8 / 10 (Non-Bt Indigenous Long Staple)
- Adaptability: Thrives in South Gujarat and parts of the Central Vadodara plains.
- Fibre Attributes: Staple length 28-30mm, spinning capability 40s count. Strong resistance to dry root rot and sucking pests. Highly stable under rainfed conditions.

2. V-797 / G.COT 21 (Herbaceum Vagad Type)
- Local Context: The historical specialty of the Viramgam, Arnej, and Bhal tracts.
- Morphology: Closed-boll type. Bolls do not open at maturity; instead, the entire boll is harvested (local term: 'Kala'). Extremely drought-tolerant, surviving on residual black-soil moisture with zero irrigation.

3. COMMERCIAL BG-II HYBRIDS
- Approved Profiles: Cultivars derived from MRC-7351, RCH-2, and ATM-2 lineages adapted to the specific day-length and soil thermal units of the Saurashtra peninsula.
