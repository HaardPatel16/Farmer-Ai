=== GROUNDNUT IRRIGATION SCHEDULING AND WATER METRICS ===
Target Zones: Summer Cultivation Belts of Saurashtra

1. CRITICAL MOISTURE PHASES
Groundnut is highly sensitive to water stress during specific intervals:
- Flowering Phase (20-25 Days Post Emergence)
- Pegging Phase (40-45 Days Post Emergence) -> Maximum Water Requirement.
- Pod Development Phase (70-80 Days Post Emergence)

2. IRRIGATION INTERVALLING USING MICRO-SPRINKLERS
- System Setup: Overhead micro-sprinklers arranged in a 6m x 6m or 9m x 9m grid layout.
- Interval Schedule: Apply water at 8-10 day intervals during March and accelerate to 6-7 day intervals during April/May when evapotranspiration rates spike. Avoid heavy flooding during peg entry to keep the topsoil loose.
