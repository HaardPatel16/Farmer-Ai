=== GROUNDNUT FERTILIZER AND CALCIUM REINFORCEMENT ===
Source Guidelines: JAU, Junagadh

1. BASAL INFUSION MATRIX
- Chemical Inputs: Apply 12.5 kg Nitrogen (N), 25 kg Phosphorus (P2O5), and 50 kg Potassium (K2O) per hectare as a basal placement dose before sowing.

2. CROP-CRITICAL GYPSUM TOP-DRESSING
- Application Strategy: Apply 250 to 400 kg of Gypsum per hectare side-dressed near root lines strictly at the pegging initiation phase (40-45 DAS). 
- Physiological Importance: Calcium is critical for shell formation and pod filling, directly eliminating empty shells (local term: 'Popsa'). Avoid applying gypsum late, as it becomes non-available to developing pegs.
