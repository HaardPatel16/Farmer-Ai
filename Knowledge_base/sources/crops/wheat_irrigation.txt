=== IRRIGATED WHEAT CRITICAL WATER MANAGEMENT ===
Source: JAU / AAU Research Data

1. WATER REQUIREMENT (CRITICAL CROWN ROOT WINDOW)
Irrigated wheat needs 5 to 6 flushes based on critical physiological phases:
- Crown Root Initiation (CRI Phase - 21 Days Post Sowing) -> Absolute Most Critical Irrigation. Failure to water here causes a 30%+ reduction in root branching.
- Tillering Initiation Phase (40-45 Days)
- Jointing Phase (60-65 Days)
- Flowering Phase (80-85 Days)
- Milking Stage (95-100 Days)
- Dough Stage (110-115 Days).

2. WATER DEPTH MAINTENANCE
- Maintain a uniform irrigation depth of 5-6 cm per flush. Avoid flooding during the dough stage to prevent lodging caused by heavy winds.
