=== GROUNDNUT VARIETAL ARCHITECTURE MATRIX ===
Database Authority: Junagadh Agricultural University (JAU)

1. GG-20 (Virginia Semi-Spreading Master Class)
- Growth Window: 115-120 days during the Kharif cycle.
- Value Profile: Features an oil content of ~51%. Displays exceptional tolerance to drought spells. The dominant variety processed in the oil mill complexes of Gondal and Jamnagar.

2. GJG-22 / GJG-32 (High Yield Bunch Selections)
- Growth Window: 100-105 days.
- Value Profile: High synchronization of pod maturity, allowing easier pull-out mechanical harvesting. Highly responsive to micro-sprinkler installations.

3. TG-37A / GJG-31 (Summer Irrigated Bunch Varieties)
- Growth Window: 105-112 days under summer heat.
- Value Profile: Features attractive bold pink kernels with smooth shells, highly valued by HPS (Hand Picked Selected) exporters operating out of the Rajkot market yards.
