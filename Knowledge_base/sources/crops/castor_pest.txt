=== CASTOR PEST DEFENCE MATRIX ===
1. CASTOR SEMI-LOOPER (Achaea janata): Larvae devour leaves quickly. Control via spraying Flubendiamide 39.35% SC @ 3 ml or Chlorantraniliprole 18.5% SC @ 4 ml per 10 liters of water.
2. CAPSULE BORER: Bores into young capsule spikes. Spray Profenophos 50% EC @ 20 ml/10L.
